Privacy Policy for DND Scheduler

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Login Credentials: Your university register number and password are transmitted securely to your university portal for authentication. We do not store these credentials on our servers or device storage.

Timetable Data: Your class schedule is downloaded from the university portal and stored locally on your device to enable DND scheduling.

App Preferences: Settings like Saturday schedule preferences and tutorial completion status are stored locally.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate with your university portal to access your timetable
  • Schedule Do Not Disturb mode during your classes
  • Display your daily class schedule
  • Remember your app preferences and settings

3. Data Security

  • All communication with university servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Login credentials are never stored - only transmitted for authentication
  • Timetable data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is shared with third parties

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties. Your data is only used within the app for its intended functionality.

5. Data Retention

Timetable data and app preferences are stored locally until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Login sessions expire automatically.

6. Your Rights

  • Clear all app data through Android Settings
  • Revoke DND and alarm permissions at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.
